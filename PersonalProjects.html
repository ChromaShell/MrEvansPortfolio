Pixel Art

A passion of mine, one that if I allowed it, would take up this entire portfolio. A fair portion of the art I do is done within the technical bounds of old computers and game consoles, not only does this give the works a unique charm but presents an endlessly interesting design challenge. Often times limited to just 4 colors and 64x64 canvas to work with, each piece becomes a puzzle of contrast and form to be solved.

Paldea Evolved

One of stylistic practice projects, the entirety of the physical'Pokemon Trading Card Game' set "Paldea Evolved" adapted into a format matching the style of the 'Pokemon Trading card Game' released in the year 2000 for the Gameboy Color, 194 cards sporting just 4 colors and a 64x48 canvas.
