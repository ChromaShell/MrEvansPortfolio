Commissions & Collaborations

This page is a collection of a handful of projects and graphics I've previously been hired to create and opportunities I've had to work with groups small and large on games and community projects, ranging from game assets to branding assets and for-print graphics.

Crystal Mines Supernova

A video game made and pusblished by Hoskbrew Games Crystal Mines Supernova is a sequel to the 1989 Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) title 'Crystal Mines.' Crystyal Mines Supernova (published in 2025) runs on the same NES hardware, now with 36 years of game design hindsight. I was tasked with bringing the character to life, creating protraits and animations within the nearly 40 year consoles technical limitations.

Mana Mania

Ive been working with Diceratops Games to bring to life their game Mana Mania (originally titled Stoicheion). This started as me just redesigning the elemental icons before they brought me on board for design consulting, ending with a successful Kickstarter funding campagin.

University of Delaware Game Jam

Though I never the attened University of Delaware I had the oppourtunity to develop the logo for their 2023 Enviromental Awareness Game Jam, designed to paralell the logo of UD's enviromantal program (featured right), the Jam focused on the enviromental impacts of our local oceans and bays. You can explore the event and all the participant's entries Here.

EvoMorphs

Evomorphs is a project by John and Evan JK Szymanski whos development has been on pause since 2022. I got to work as both a sprite/concept artist and design consultant on, as well as adapting various previous designs and sprites to match the games new style. You can find more here!
