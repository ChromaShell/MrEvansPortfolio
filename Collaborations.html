Collaborations

This page is a collection of some of the many opportunities I've had to work with groups small and large on games and community projects.

Mana Mania

Ive been working with Diceratops Games to bring to life their game Mana Mania (originally titled Stoicheion). This started as me just redesigning the elemental icons before they brought me on board for design consulting, ending with a successful Kickstarter funding campagin.

University of Delaware Game Jam

Though I never the attened University of Delaware I had the oppourtunity to develop the logo for their 2023 Enviromental Awareness Game Jam, designed to paralell the logo of UD's enviromantal program (featured right), the Jam focused on the enviromental impacts of our local oceans and bays.
